Scoring rules

Picks

For each NFL playoff game, entrants select:

  • Advancing Team
  • Spread Winner
  • Over / Under

Picks are submitted weekly. Super Bowl predictions are submitted once before the playoffs begin.

Default picks for not submitting will be away team, underdog, and under. If you submit more than once, the last submission is used.

Scoring by Round

Wild Card Round

  • 3 points – Advancing Team
  • 2 points – Spread Winner
  • 1 point – Over / Under

Divisional Round

  • 4.5 points – Advancing Team
  • 3 points – Spread Winner
  • 1.5 points – Over / Under

Conference Championships

  • 9 points – Advancing Team
  • 6 points – Spread Winner
  • 3 points – Over / Under

Super Bowl

  • 13.5 points – Advancing Team
  • 9 points – Spread Winner
  • 4.5 points – Over / Under

Super Bowl Predictions

  • 4.5 points – Correct AFC Champion
  • 4.5 points – Correct NFC Champion
  • 1.5 points – Predict both teams correctly (bonus)
  • 7.5 points – Correct Super Bowl Champion

Tie-Breaker

Cumulative total of the point differential (against the spread) between your selection and the team they are playing. Losing selections earn points (bad). Winning selections earn 0 points (good). Lower tie-breaker is better.
